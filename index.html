My Development Timeline

A journey through my evolution in coding, design, and community building.

2019

Started my journey into development — experimenting with Lua andPython scripts for fun and curiosity, learning the basics of logic and automation.

2020

Discovered Roblox scripting and Minecraft plugins. Created small ESPs, CFrame aimlocks, and bots — early steps into game modification and automation.

2021

Launched the alias Drexxy and began Drexware — focusing on external cheats and sleek ImGui-based UIs. Learned memory manipulation, offsets, and advanced Lua logic.

2022

Refined design skills, crafting UIs inspired by Neverlose andPrimordial. Experimented with rendering APIs for ESPs and introduced complex anti-aim systems. Managed the CLOUD server community.

2023

Ventured into CS2 development. Built Python tools like memory dumpers and HWID spoofers. Enhanced cheat logic with dynamic offsets, clantags, and advanced anti-aim builders.

Early 2025

Focused heavily on design & branding. Built HTML/CSS-based UIs, polished Lua scripts, and redefined my online identity to match modern aesthetics.

August 2025

Rebranded from Drexxy to Cloud. Embraced clean, minimalistic visuals and began focusing on public projects with strong identity and presentation.

September 2025

Closed the CLOUD server after 2 years of activity. This closure marked a transition toward more refined community management and brand clarity.

October 2025

Launched the Advantage server with a vision for new opportunities. Soon after, rebranded to VERZE and expanded into professional-grade graphic design, HTML websites, and digital branding.
